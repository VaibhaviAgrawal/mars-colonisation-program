Instructions

  1. Create Table by clicking on "Create a Grid" button
  2. Mark starting point. First click on the button "Mark Starting Point" then click on the cell which you want to make the Starting point. It will be converted to a red cell after that.
  3. Mark ending point. Click on the button "Mark Ending Point" and then click on the cell which you want to make as ending point. It will be converted to a green cell after that.
  4. Mark barrier points. Click on the button "Mark Barrier Points" and then click on the cells you want to make as barriers. It will be converted to a grey cell after that.
  5. Click on any one of the Path Finding Algorithm from the list.
  6. Press "Clear" before restarting the game.
Note: Everytime before starting new search, one needs to create the table from starting.
Cheers!
